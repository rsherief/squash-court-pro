# Squash Court Pro – Project Guidelines

## Project Purpose
This is a **test sandbox for SEO optimization and content editing** using Claude. It serves as a learning environment for testing content strategies, keyword integration, and content quality standards.

## Brand Tone
- **Professional yet conversational**: Authoritative expertise without corporate stiffness
- Balance technical accuracy with approachable language
- Speak to athletes as peers, not customers
- Use active voice; avoid jargon unless necessary

## Primary Keywords
- `enterprise data security` (test keyword)
- `cloud compliance` (test keyword)
- Secondary: squash equipment, Riyadh, professional rackets, court setup

## Content Quality Standards: Google E-E-A-T

All content updates must adhere to **Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness**:

### Expertise (E)
- Content should demonstrate deep knowledge of the subject
- Back claims with data, research, or professional credentials
- For product pages: include specs, comparisons, use cases

### Experience (E)
- Share real-world context and practical insights
- Include perspectives from actual athletes/coaches when possible
- Avoid generic advice; provide specific, tested recommendations

### Authoritativeness (A)
- Source claims from reputable industry sources
- Build author credibility through consistent, accurate information
- Link to trusted references and certifications

### Trustworthiness (T)
- Be transparent about product limitations and pricing
- Clearly disclose any affiliations or commercial interests
- Use verified customer reviews and testimonials
- Maintain consistent, honest brand messaging

## Content Update Workflow

1. **Plan**: Define keyword targets and E-E-A-T approach
2. **Draft**: Write in brand tone; incorporate primary keywords naturally
3. **Review**: Verify expertise claims and cite sources
4. **Optimize**: Ensure headings, metadata, and internal links support SEO
5. **Publish**: Deploy and monitor performance

## Testing Guidelines

- Test keyword integration without forced placement
- A/B test headlines and CTAs
- Monitor bounce rate, time-on-page, and conversion metrics
- Document what works for future reference
- Iterate based on performance data
