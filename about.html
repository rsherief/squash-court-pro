About Squash Court Pro

Your partner in mastering the game

Our Story

Squash Court Pro was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring world-class squash equipment to athletes across the Middle East. What started as a small passion project has grown into Riyadh's premier destination for professional squash gear.

We believe that every player—from beginners learning the fundamentals to elite athletes competing at the highest level—deserves access to premium equipment and expert guidance. Our carefully curated selection combines international brands with local expertise, ensuring that every racket, ball, and accessory meets our rigorous standards.

Today, we serve hundreds of players throughout Saudi Arabia and beyond, with a dedicated showroom in central Riyadh and a commitment to fast, reliable delivery across the kingdom.
